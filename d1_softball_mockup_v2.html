This Week in Softball February 3, 2025

Legends Stay Hot as Playoff Race Heats Up

The Legends extended their winning streak to six games with a convincing 14-8 victory over the Thunder on Tuesday. Meanwhile, the Renegades are lurking just two games back after their sweep of the Pioneers.

With three weeks left in the regular season, six teams are still mathematically alive for the four playoff spots. Key matchups this week include Legends vs. Renegades on Thursday (Knudson, 8 AM) - a game that could go a long way toward determining home field advantage.

Reminder: The rain makeup game between Storm and Heat has been rescheduled to Friday at Saddlebrook. Check the schedule page for updated times.
